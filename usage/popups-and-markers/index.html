Linking Popups & Markers

You can have a popup linked to a marker by simply nesting the popup component inside the marker. Example:

    <MapboxDefaultMarker 
      marker-id="marker1"
      :options="{}"
      :lnglat="[110, 5]"
    >
      <MapboxDefaultPopup
        popup-id="popup1"
        :lnglat="[100, 0]"
        :options="{
          closeOnClick: false
        }"
      >
        <h1 class="test">
          Hello World!
        </h1>
      </MapboxDefaultPopup>
    </MapboxDefaultMarker>
